Lets find out together why digital Marketing is so important nowadays


New products and services are introduced to the mass public through advertising. Advertising holds immense importance and exists in all media platforms such as TV, print and especially social media. Although several internet users block ads to appear on the screen, almost half of the people make get attracted to the products they see in ads and they tend to buy them.
Unfortunately, many people confuse the term digital marketing with online marketing. In fact, there are a huge number of differences between them that you should be aware of. Undoubtedly, digital marketing includes online marketing, but it is much broader.
Internet marketing today is SEO, contextual advertising and much more. We are talking about resources that are only on the expanses of the World Wide Web. If we talk about digital marketing, then all of the above should be added to advertising above and promoted on digital media hosted outside the network. It turns out that we are talking about digital communications not only online, but also offline.

If we talk about advertising attracted to digital, then ads in print media, advertising on television or on billboards will not apply to it. But if the billboard contains a QR code that, when read, makes it difficult to read on the company's website, then this advertisement can already correspond to digital marketing.
